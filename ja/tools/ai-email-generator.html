Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI write professional emails?

Yes, AI can write highly professional emails by utilizing advanced language templates and formatting rules. It ensures proper grammar, appropriate tone, and clear structure, making it ideal for business correspondence.

How do I write a formal email using AI?

To write a formal email, simply input your key points, select 'Formal & Polite' as the tone, and specify the purpose (e.g., Business Inquiry). The AI will structure the email with a professional greeting, clear body, and formal sign-off.

Is it safe to generate emails using online AI tools?

Yes, our AI email generator uses privacy-first processing. All text generation happens within your browser using local templates and algorithms, meaning your sensitive email data is never sent to external servers.

What are the best tones for business emails?

For business emails, 'Professional' and 'Formal & Polite' are the best tones. Professional tone is great for daily communication with colleagues, while Formal is best for reaching out to new clients, executives, or formal job applications.

How can I write an email requesting a meeting?

Select the 'Meeting Request' purpose, provide your recipient's name, and include details like the desired date, time, and topic in the 'Key Points' section. The AI will generate a clear, concise request that respects the recipient's time.
