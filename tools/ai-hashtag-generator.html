Frequently Asked Questions

How many hashtags should I use on Instagram?

Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post, but recent algorithm updates suggest that using 3 to 5 highly relevant hashtags is optimal for maximizing reach and minimizing spam signals.

How do hashtags increase social media reach?

Hashtags categorize your content. When users search for or follow specific hashtags, your post can appear in their feeds even if they don't follow your account, drastically expanding your organic reach.

What is the difference between popular and niche hashtags?

Popular hashtags (e.g., #love, #business) have millions of posts and high competition, meaning your post might get lost quickly. Niche hashtags (e.g., #AustinVeganBakery) have lower search volume but highly targeted, engaged audiences.

Can I use the same hashtags for LinkedIn and TikTok?

It is not recommended. LinkedIn favors professional, industry-specific tags (e.g., #Leadership, #SaaS), whereas TikTok relies on viral, entertainment-driven, and trend-focused tags (e.g., #fyp, #TechTok). Tailor your tags to the platform.

How do I copy all hashtags at once?

After generating your tailored hashtag list with our tool, simply click the 'Copy All' button at the top right of the result box to copy them directly to your clipboard for easy pasting into your social media scheduler.
