About the Area Converter

This area converter helps you easily switch between various units of two-dimensional space measurement. Area calculations are essential for real estate, land development, farming, interior design, and many other industries where understanding the size of a surface is necessary.

Key area conversions to keep in mind include: 1 square meter (m²) is approximately 10.7639 square feet (ft²); 1 acre equals 4,046.86 m²; and 1 hectare is exactly 10,000 m², which translates to roughly 2.471 acres. For large land masses, 1 square kilometer (km²) is equal to 0.386102 square miles (mi²).

Whether you are determining the square footage of a new apartment, measuring a plot of agricultural land in hectares, or planning a home renovation project, this tool provides quick, accurate results. Just select your starting and target units, and let the calculator do the math for you.

What is a hectare in acres?

How many square miles are in a square kilometer?

How do I convert square feet to square yards?

To convert square feet to square yards, divide the number of square feet by 9. Since 1 yard is 3 feet, 1 square yard is 3x3 = 9 square feet.
