What is Lorem Ipsum and why is it used?

Lorem Ipsum is standard dummy text used in the printing and typesetting industry. It is used as a placeholder to demonstrate the visual form of a document or typeface without relying on meaningful content. This prevents readers from being distracted by the text itself, allowing them to focus on the layout, font, and overall design.

The standard Lorem Ipsum passage is heavily derived from Cicero's 'De finibus bonorum et malorum' (On the Ends of Good and Evil), written in 45 BC.
